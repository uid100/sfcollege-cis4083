Industry Survey

Purpose:

Explore the industry and marketplace, identify the leading Cloud Service Providers, and discuss your findings.

You probably know at least some of these already. Use your favorite search method to discover something about the leading cloud service providers to find answers to the prompts.

 

Directions:

COMPLETE YOUR INITIAL POST AT LEAST 24 HOURS BEFORE THE DEADLINE SO THAT OTHERS MAY REPLY

  1. (25 pts) Click reply below, and share your findings on the following.
    Your post should contain well constructed paragraphs with correct grammar and spelling.
    Use headings to separate the sections of your post.
    • List the top 10 cloud service providers.
      (This can be in a bulleted list, separate from your paragraph(s))
    • Is this list global data or within the United States?
    • What was the source for this data? Did you compare others?
    • What is the criteria for determining this ranking (e.g. marketshare)?
    • How recent are these statistics?
    • How many of these providers were already familiar to you?
    • Did you learn any one (or more) facts about some of these companies that was new to you?
  2. (25 pts) Visit websites for (at least) the top 3 from your list.
    • Are their websites clear and easy to navigate?
    • Do they offer free or discounted plans for getting started?
    • Is it clear what services they offer and how to get started?
    Did you have a favorite that you would recommend or like to revisit?

     

    DON'T FORGET TO COME BACK AND REPLY TO OTHERS
  3. (25 pts) Respond to two or more other posts
    • Did their findings match yours?
    • Did they cite the same sources?
    • Were there new, unfamiliar names on these other lists?
    • Comparing your findings with others, what stood out to you?
    • Is there general consensus within the top 5? 3? 2? 1?

Posts and replies should be thoughtful and respectful.
