Create an AWS Account

Follow these steps to create your personal AWS account for coursework.

Before You Begin: What You Need

  • Valid Email Address (used for login and verification)
  • Phone Number (for SMS or voice verification)
  • Credit or Debit Card (required for identity verification)
  • Personal Information (name and address)

Step-by-Step Account Creation

1. Access the AWS Signup Page

Go to: https://portal.aws.amazon.com/billing/signup
Click Create an AWS Account.

2. Enter Email & Account Name

  1. Enter your email address.
  2. Create an AWS account name (e.g., your name or project name).
  3. Check your email for a verification code and enter it to continue.

3. Create Your Password

Set a secure password for the AWS root user.

4. Provide Contact Information

  • Select Personal as the account type.
  • Enter your full name, address, and country.
  • Agree to the AWS Customer Agreement and continue.

5. Add a Payment Method

Enter your credit/debit card details. AWS may place a temporary authorization hold for verification. If you stay within free tier limits, you will not be billed.

6. Verify Your Identity

Choose SMS or Voice Call and enter the verification code sent to your phone.

7. Select a Support Plan

Choose the Basic Support Plan (Free).

8. Complete Signup and Wait for Activation

AWS will activate your account. This may take a few minutes up to 24 hours. Look for a confirmation email once complete.

After Your Account Is Active

Secure the Account

Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) on the root account (recommended).

Monitor Usage and Billing

Use the Billing Dashboard to:

  • Track Free Tier usage
  • Enable Billing Alerts
  • Prevent unexpected charges
