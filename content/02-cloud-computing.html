This definition describes highly available delivery of IT services. In the last module, I included a video in which included a discussion of cloud computing services in terms of three layers, Software, Infrastructure and Platform. If you skipped over this, or just want to watch it again (6 minutes) it is repeated below.

 

These layers commonly map to service types or categories. Specifically Infrastructure-as-a-Service (IaaS), Software-as-a-Service (SaaS), Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS). Sometimes, collectively these cloud computing services are referred to as Anything-as-a-Service and abbreviated (XaaS).
