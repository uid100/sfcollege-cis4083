Module 10: Migrating to the Cloud

Disruption

According to Business Leader online, an industry Disruptor is a service or product which replaces or displaces a conventional market practice. Not too long ago, shops in every major city offered video tape and eventually disc format movie rentals. These have become obsolete. A small niche coffee shop in Seattle took a unique business model to international success. Even takeout from local small restaurants, in more and more cases, have menus and even ordering hosted online. Stop for a moment to think about how many things we do every day that have replaced the way it used to be.

Listen to Matthias Farwick, the CEO and founder of Txture, a cloud systems integrator, share his thoughts on the direction and impact on cloud data systems.
