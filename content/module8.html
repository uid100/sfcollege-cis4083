Overview

IDaaS stands for Identity-as-a-Service, which is a cloud-based identity management solution that enables organizations to manage digital identities securely and efficiently.

IDaaS provides a centralized platform that allows businesses to manage access control, authentication, authorization, and identity governance for their employees, customers, partners, and other stakeholders.

IDaaS providers typically offer a range of features and capabilities, including single sign-on (SSO), multi-factor authentication (MFA), directory services, user provisioning, access control, and identity analytics.

By using IDaaS, organizations can improve security, streamline access management, and reduce the costs associated with managing on-premise identity infrastructure.
